\# Operational Intelligence and Signal Engine



Status: Draft



\---



\# Objective



Define the operational intelligence architecture responsible for deriving runtime signals, coordinator burden analysis, workflow risk detection, protocol complexity scoring, deviation prediction, and operational oversight intelligence from immutable runtime execution.



\---



\# Core Principle



Operational intelligence emerges continuously from execution runtime.



The system computes operational reality from events, workflows, protocol logic, and longitudinal execution patterns.



\---



\# Responsibilities



The Operational Intelligence and Signal Engine must support:



\- coordinator burden analysis

\- workflow friction analysis

\- protocol complexity scoring

\- operational risk scoring

\- unresolved workflow detection

\- deviation prediction

\- monitoring risk signals

\- safety escalation signals

\- amendment impact intelligence

\- query burden analysis



\---

\## Coordinator Signals

\- coordinator\_overload\_risk

\- documentation\_backlog

\- visit\_collision\_risk

\- dependency\_complexity\_score



\---



\## Safety Signals

\---



\## Governance Signals



Examples:

\- repeated\_monitoring\_findings



\---



\## Financial Signals



Examples:

\- leakage\_probability

\- uninvoiced\_work\_risk

\- amendment\_underbudget\_risk

\- coordinator\_burden\_underpayment



\---



\# Signal Inputs



Signals derive from:



\- immutable operational events

\- protocol graph

\- visit runtime

\- procedure execution

\- source completion

\- safety workflows

\- deviations

\- CAPAs

\- monitoring findings

\- financial runtime



\---



\# Protocol Complexity Scoring



The engine must compute protocol complexity using factors such as:



\- branch density

\- procedure count

\- conditional workflows

\- repeated assessments

\- safety escalation frequency

\- amendment frequency

\- dependency graph depth

\- off-site workflow complexity



\---



\# Coordinator Burden Intelligence



The engine must quantify:



\- active workload

\- unresolved workflow load

\- context switching burden

\- documentation burden

\- safety burden

\- query burden

\- amendment burden



\---



\# Workflow Friction Intelligence



The system must detect:



\- repeated workflow bottlenecks

\- unresolved dependency chains

\- delayed visit progression

\- repeated source incompleteness

\- repeated missing signatures

\- operational stall patterns



\---



\# Deviation Prediction



The engine should support early detection of:



\- probable protocol deviations

\- overdue workflows

\- visit window risk

\- unresolved safety carry-forward

\- blocked procedures

\- workflow abandonment risk



\---



\# Longitudinal Intelligence



The engine must support:



\- subject continuity analysis

\- study-level operational trends

\- coordinator workload trends

\- protocol execution trends

\- amendment impact evolution



\---



\# UX Requirements



The coordinator workspace must surface:



\- what is at risk

\- why it is at risk

\- urgency level

\- next recommended action

\- unresolved blockers



without exposing analytical complexity.



\---



\# AI Integration



AI may assist with:



\- anomaly detection

\- signal clustering

\- trend summarization

\- operational recommendations



AI must remain explainable and event-grounded.



\---



\# MVP Scope



MVP must support:



\- workload signals

\- protocol complexity scoring

\- unresolved workflow signals

\- basic deviation risk detection

\- basic financial leakage signals

\- operational alert prioritization



\---



\# Deferred Scope



Deferred:



\- autonomous operational decision making

\- advanced predictive AI

\- enterprise-wide benchmarking marketplace

\- black-box risk engines



\---



\# Explicit Non-Goals



The engine is NOT:



\- generic BI dashboarding

\- vanity analytics

\- sponsor KPI reporting layer

\- disconnected AI recommendation engine



\---



\# Final Rule



If operational intelligence cannot be explained from runtime events and protocol logic, the intelligence architecture is invalid.
