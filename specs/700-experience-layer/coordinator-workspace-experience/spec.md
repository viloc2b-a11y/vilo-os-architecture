\# Coordinator Workspace Experience Layer



Status: Draft



\---



\# Objective



Define the coordinator-first experience layer that serves as the operational entry point to Vilo OS across site, study, subject, visit, source, safety, regulatory, financial, and workflow execution.



\---



\# Core Principle



The coordinator should not navigate modules.



The system should surface the next operational action.



\---



\# Responsibilities



The Coordinator Workspace must support:



\- site-level operational overview

\- study-level execution status

\- subject-level longitudinal view

\- visit-level execution workspace

\- procedure-level action guidance

\- unresolved safety visibility

\- regulatory readiness visibility

\- source completion visibility

\- workflow prioritization

\- financial/revenue signals

\- operational blockers



\---



\# Experience Model



The workspace organizes work by:



1\. Site

2\. Study

3\. Subject

4\. Visit

5\. Procedure

6\. Workflow

7\. Exception



NOT by disconnected modules.



\---



\# Primary UX Surfaces



\## Site Command Center



Shows:

\- active studies

\- today’s visits

\- overdue visits

\- safety alerts

\- incomplete source

\- open deviations

\- revenue leakage alerts

\- regulatory blockers



\---



\## Study Workspace



Shows:

\- enrolled subjects

\- visit schedule

\- protocol status

\- amendment status

\- visit readiness

\- unresolved workflows

\- study-level risk

\- study-level revenue signals



\---



\## Subject Timeline



Shows:

\- screening status

\- randomization status

\- visit history

\- upcoming visits

\- safety history

\- con meds

\- medical history

\- deviations

\- source completeness

\- financial traceability



\---



\## Visit Workspace



Shows:

\- visit readiness

\- required procedures

\- conditional procedures

\- source sections

\- signatures

\- unresolved blockers

\- protocol guidance

\- financial impact

\- safety dependencies



\---



\## Exception Queue



Shows:

\- overdue workflows

\- missing signatures

\- unresolved AEs

\- open deviations

\- query burden

\- protocol window risk

\- unpaid earned work



\---



\# Coordinator Guidance



The system must continuously answer:



\- What do I need to do now?

\- What is blocking this visit?

\- What is missing?

\- What changed?

\- What is at risk?

\- What needs PI review?

\- What should be billed?



\---



\# Cognitive Load Reduction



The workspace must reduce:



\- protocol lookup burden

\- cross-module navigation

\- manual checklist tracking

\- duplicated documentation

\- spreadsheet reconciliation

\- memory-dependent follow-up



\---



\# Runtime Integration



The workspace consumes:



\- visit runtime state

\- protocol graph state

\- eSource completion state

\- safety state

\- financial signals

\- governance signals

\- operational events



\---



\# UX Rules



The workspace must:



\- preserve context

\- minimize clicks

\- avoid buried actions

\- prioritize urgent work

\- show blockers clearly

\- explain why something is required

\- connect actions to protocol logic



\---



\# MVP Scope



MVP must support:



\- site dashboard

\- study dashboard

\- subject timeline

\- visit workspace

\- task/exception queue

\- safety visibility

\- source completion visibility

\- basic revenue signals



\---



\# Deferred Scope



Deferred:



\- sponsor dashboards

\- CRO command centers

\- advanced enterprise analytics

\- marketplace widgets

\- unnecessary personalization engines



\---



\# Explicit Non-Goals



The workspace is NOT:



\- a generic dashboard

\- a static CTMS home page

\- a sponsor reporting layer

\- a disconnected module launcher



\---



\# Final Rule



If the coordinator needs to remember where to go next, the experience layer has failed.
