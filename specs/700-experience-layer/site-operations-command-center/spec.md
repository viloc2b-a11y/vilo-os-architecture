\# Site Operations Command Center



Status: Draft



\---



\# Objective



Define the site-level operational command center that provides a unified execution view across studies, subjects, visits, safety, regulatory, staffing workload, revenue, and operational risk.



\---



\# Core Principle



The site is the base operating unit.



Vilo OS must help the site run better every day.



\---



\# Responsibilities



The command center must surface:



\- active studies

\- today’s visits

\- overdue visits

\- visit readiness

\- open safety items

\- incomplete source

\- regulatory blockers

\- open deviations

\- open queries

\- coordinator workload

\- revenue leakage

\- operational risk signals



\---



\# Core Views



\## Today View



Shows:

\- scheduled visits

\- ready visits

\- blocked visits

\- missing procedures

\- safety holds

\- staffing conflicts



\## Study View



Shows:

\- study status

\- enrollment status

\- visit execution status

\- source completion

\- regulatory readiness

\- financial signals



\## Exception View



Shows:

\- overdue workflows

\- unresolved AEs

\- missing signatures

\- open deviations

\- open queries

\- unpaid earned work



\## Revenue View



Shows:

\- earned revenue

\- missed billables

\- invoice-ready items

\- underpaid workload

\- amendment impact



\---



\# Runtime Inputs



The command center consumes:



\- visit runtime

\- subject timeline

\- operational events

\- safety engine

\- regulatory engine

\- financial engine

\- governance signals

\- recruitment signals



\---



\# UX Requirements



The site team must answer:



\- What needs attention today?

\- What is blocked?

\- What is at risk?

\- What is missing?

\- What has been earned?

\- What needs escalation?



\---



\# MVP Scope



MVP must support:



\- site dashboard

\- today’s visit list

\- exception queue

\- study overview

\- safety alerts

\- source gaps

\- basic revenue leakage signals



\---



\# Deferred Scope



Deferred:



\- sponsor dashboards

\- CRO oversight dashboards

\- enterprise benchmarking

\- external BI replacement



\---



\# Explicit Non-Goals



The command center is NOT:



\- generic dashboard

\- sponsor reporting homepage

\- vanity analytics page

\- disconnected KPI wall



\---



\# Final Rule



If the site cannot tell what to do today from this screen, the command center has failed.
