\# MVP Boundary and Enterprise Roadmap



Status: Draft



\---



\# Objective



Define the MVP boundary, deferred enterprise roadmap, exclusion rules, and execution sequence for Vilo OS.



\---



\# Core Principle



Build runtime first.



Everything else comes after operational truth exists.



\---

\- Operational event spine



\## Protocol Execution

\- Visit matrix

\- Runtime publication



\## eSource



\- Dynamic source sections

\- Signatures

\- Addenda



\## Safety

\- SAE workflow

\- Safety-linked blockers



\## Governance

\- Deviation tracking

\- Query/finding tracking

\- Audit reconstruction



\## Finance



\- Expected billables

\- Billable instances

\- Earned revenue

\- Missed billable signals

\- Invoice readiness



\## Experience



\- Site command center

\- Study workspace

\- Subject timeline

\- Visit workspace

\- Exception queue



\---



\# MVP Must NOT Include



\- Sponsor portal

\- CRO command center

\- Enterprise marketplace

\- Full eTMF replacement

\- Full LIMS replacement

\- Full ERP/accounting replacement

\- Autonomous AI agents

\- Complex visual rule builder

\- External integration layer first

\- Enterprise SSO first

\# Enterprise Roadmap



Deferred but planned:



\- external EDC sync

\- accounting integration

\- LIMS integration

\- eTMF integration

\- sponsor-safe visibility

\- CRO-safe exports

\- enterprise analytics

\- predictive risk models

\- advanced negotiation intelligence

\- automated protocol extraction



\---



\# Exclusion Rules



Reject any feature that:



\- increases coordinator cognitive burden

\- duplicates disconnected workflows

\- requires manual reconciliation

\- starts sponsor-first

\- creates fake AI automation

\- introduces unnecessary microservices

\- turns Vilo OS into generic CTMS



\---



\# Final Rule



If the MVP cannot prove runtime execution, governance lineage, and revenue traceability, it is not the MVP.
