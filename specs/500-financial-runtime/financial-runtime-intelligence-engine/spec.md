\# Financial Runtime Intelligence Engine



Status: Draft



\---



\# Objective



Define the financial intelligence engine that derives revenue, leakage, billables, workload value, amendment impact, and negotiation evidence from operational runtime execution.



\---



\# Core Principle



Finance emerges from execution truth.



Budgets are not enough. Runtime proves value.



\---



\# Responsibilities



The Financial Runtime Intelligence Engine must support:



\- expected billables

\- executed billables

\- earned revenue

\- missed revenue

\- invoice readiness

\- payment reconciliation

\- dispute tracking

\- revenue leakage detection

\- coordinator workload valuation

\- amendment impact analysis

\- negotiation evidence generation



\---



\# Financial Runtime Chain



Protocol Requirement

→ Expected Billable

→ Visit Execution

→ Procedure Completion

→ Operational Event

→ Earned Revenue

→ Invoice Readiness

→ Payment Tracking

→ Leakage Detection

→ Negotiation Evidence



\---



\# Core Financial Entities



\- Expected Billable

\- Billable Instance

\- Earned Revenue

\- Missed Revenue Signal

\- Invoice Item

\- Payment Record

\- Dispute

\- Amendment Impact

\- Workload Signal

\- Negotiation Evidence



\---



\# Expected Billables



Expected billables derive from:



\- protocol graph

\- visit definitions

\- procedure definitions

\- study budget

\- contract terms

\- amendment deltas



\---



\# Executed Billables



Executed billables derive from:



\- procedure completion

\- visit completion

\- safety workflow execution

\- unscheduled visits

\- additional protocol-required work

\- source completion

\- operational events



\---



\# Revenue Leakage Detection



The system must detect:



\- completed but uninvoiced procedures

\- unscheduled work not billed

\- safety work not billed

\- amendment work not reflected in budget

\- coordinator burden not compensated

\- missed pass-throughs

\- procedure performed without mapped billable



\---



\# Amendment Impact



The engine must compute:



\- new procedures

\- removed procedures

\- visit burden changes

\- safety burden changes

\- source burden changes

\- training burden changes

\- monitoring burden changes

\- revenue delta

\- staffing impact



\---



\# Workload Valuation



The engine must quantify:



\- coordinator time burden

\- query burden

\- safety follow-up burden

\- rescheduling burden

\- amendment burden

\- protocol complexity burden



\---



\# Negotiation Intelligence



The engine must produce:



\- sponsor-facing justification

\- site burden evidence

\- protocol complexity evidence

\- underpayment signals

\- counteroffer support

\- amendment budget defense



\---



\# Operational Event Integration



Financial signals must be derived from immutable events such as:



\- procedure\_completed

\- visit\_locked

\- unscheduled\_visit\_created

\- safety\_escalated

\- query\_opened

\- source\_signed

\- amendment\_applied



\---



\# UX Requirements



The site must see:



\- what has been earned

\- what is missing

\- what is blocked

\- what is underpaid

\- what should be invoiced

\- what should be negotiated



Without manually reconciling spreadsheets.



\---



\# MVP Scope



MVP must support:



\- expected billables

\- billable instances

\- procedure-to-billable linkage

\- earned revenue signals

\- missed billable signals

\- invoice readiness view

\- basic leakage detection



\---



\# Deferred Scope



Deferred:



\- full accounting system replacement

\- automatic sponsor invoicing

\- ERP integration

\- payment processor integration

\- advanced contract lifecycle management



\---



\# Explicit Non-Goals



The engine is NOT:



\- QuickBooks

\- ERP

\- sponsor payment portal

\- static budget spreadsheet

\- manual reconciliation tool



\---



\# Final Rule



If site revenue cannot be traced back to runtime execution, the financial architecture is invalid.
